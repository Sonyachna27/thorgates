About the Company

Thorgates is a trusted aluminum contractor in Los Angeles, California, specializing in the design, supply, and installation of aluminum gates, fences, doors, and siding. We provide complete turnkey solutions for homeowners and businesses who want modern design, durability, and low-maintenance aluminum products.

Our team focuses on delivering projects that combine style, strength, and long-lasting value. Whether it’s a custom aluminum driveway gate, a secure fence, or stylish siding, Thorgates ensures professional installation and outstanding quality.

Durable Materials — Rust-free, weather-resistant aluminum.
Turnkey Service — From design to installation, we handle everything.
Custom Designs — Tailored to match your property’s style and needs.
